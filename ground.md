# Definition

1.  The earth's surface, especially the earth's land surface. Used in
    combination to form adjectives, as in ground-to-air,
    ground-to-ground , and air-to-ground. See surface. 2. The domain of
    nonflight operations that normally take place on the earth's surface
    or in a vehicle or on a platform that rests upon the surface, as in
    ground support. 3. = electrical ground.
